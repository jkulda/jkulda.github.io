About

Author

The MPtools package is being conceived, developed and maintained by Jiri Kulda, instrument group leader emeritus at the Institut Laue-Langevin, Grenoble, France. Be encouraged to help improving MPtools by contributing questions, comments, suggestions as well as bug reports to the email address related to this site. If you find this library useful, or it helps you in creating software or publications, please let me know and acknowledge MPtools by citing this repository and the corresponding journal papers (to come).
